Ohio State Habitat for Humanity

Habitat for Humanity at Ohio State seeks to provide Ohio State students with the opportunity to give back to the Columbus community, advocate on the issue of substandard housing, and educate the Ohio State student body on the issue of substandard housing and poverty in America.
