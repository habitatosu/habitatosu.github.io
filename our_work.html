what do we do?

Our organization works to reduce the problem of substandard housing in Columbus and Abroad. Working directly with Habitat for Humanity International and Mid-Ohio we provide volunteers to work on building homes for the families that need it most. This year we have been able to send over 50 volunteers to home building sites in Columbus and 20 volunteers to Salem, North Carolina and Des Moines, Iowa. In addition to service work we fund raise on campus and advocate for the issues of poverty and substandard housing. Essentially, our organization works to help people in any way that we possibly can.

Travel

2 yearly trips to Habitat affiliates, be on the lookout for dates and times!

Advocate

Advocate for the cause of substandard housing in the United States and abroad during our monthly meetings/events

Build

Bi-weekly build days where we work on homes in Columbus, OH, including trips to the H4H restore, Franklington Gardens, and others.
